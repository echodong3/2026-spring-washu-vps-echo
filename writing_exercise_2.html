I think the installation at the back of Olin library bring out the best in me because it is very soothing to look at it when I'm feeling stressed or overwhelmed. It is an installation that measure the speed of wind and there is a group of vertical reflective stands that form an interesting pattern. I just think it's a beautiful state of art and allow me to ponder at things beyond everyday stress, like how fast the wind is and how the individual thing rotates with wind. The reflective nature of the material also showcase the weather like reflecting bright sunlight or the color of the sunset, which looks very beautiful.
