About

What are constellations?

Constellations are recognizable groups or regions of stars in the sky. They help viewers orient themselves in the heavens, but they also carry meanings shaped by culture, memory, and storytelling.

What are zodiac signs?

The zodiac is the band of sky along the ecliptic. It is associated with a sequence of constellations connected to the yearly motion of the Sun.

How to use this website

Start on the home page, move into the zodiac map, then open constellation pages to read the overview, locate the main stars, and focus on the starlore section.

Design goal

The goal is to keep the interface simple, modern, dark, and clean while giving the story and symbolism of each constellation stronger visual emphasis.
