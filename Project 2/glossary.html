Glossary

Technical and interpretive terms used throughout the project.

Constellation

A recognized grouping or region of stars in the sky.

Zodiac

The band of sky along the ecliptic associated with zodiac constellations and signs.

Ecliptic

The apparent path of the Sun across the sky through the year.

Magnitude

A measure of a star’s apparent brightness.

Asterism

A familiar star pattern that is not officially a full constellation.

Celestial Sphere

An imaginary sphere around Earth onto which stars appear projected.

Starlore

Stories, myths, symbolic meanings, and cultural interpretations attached to the stars.
